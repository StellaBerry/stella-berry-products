The policy is to let purchasers use it as freely as possible for their own creative activities, but distributing, reposting, or selling the distribution files themselves without permission is prohibited.

Generated text, screenshots, and settings or notes you entered or edited yourself may be used for personal use, publishing, posting, streaming, doujin activities, and commercial or non-commercial creative activities.

Use is permitted for both individuals and corporations, provided the software was obtained legitimately. When streaming with this software, you may monetize your streams through each streaming platform's official monetization features. When doing so, please credit this software in the stream description or a similar place.
