The screen for managing **partner characters**, who join timeline posts, replies, and DM conversations.

<Image src={shot} alt="Followers screen" class="rg-screenshot" />

- Set each partner's **profile, tone, likes, and relationship notes**.
- Add and edit partners to broaden your interactions.
- You can also **pick a DM partner and start a conversation** from here.

:::tip
Tuning the tone and relationship notes makes replies feel more like that character. You can also refine writing style with [Analysis](/stella-berry-products/en/role-garden-webui/basics/analysis/).
:::
