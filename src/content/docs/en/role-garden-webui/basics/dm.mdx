The screen for one-on-one conversations with partner characters — the heart of roleplay.

<Image src={shot} alt="DM screen" class="rg-screenshot" />

- Beyond dialogue, it supports text with **movement, expressions, distance, gaze, and scene descriptions**.
- After you send a message, the partner replies after a short while, showing "typing…" first rather than instantly.
- The more you talk, the more the character remembers about you.

:::note
For how memory works, see [How It Works](/stella-berry-products/en/role-garden-webui/how-it-works/).
:::
