For Windows 64-bit. No tricky configuration needed — **just extract the zip and double-click the launcher**.

## Setup steps

<Steps>

1. Extract the distribution zip anywhere you like.
2. Double-click **`Role Garden WebUI Launcher.exe`**.
3. The launcher prepares the right models for your PC and starts the app.

</Steps>

On first run, simply follow the on-screen guide: it checks your PC specs, lets you choose where to store data, and downloads the models with a progress bar. Basically, all you do is "double-click" and "confirm the models".

## What you need

- A Windows 64-bit PC
- An internet connection for the initial setup

## What the setup prepares automatically

- Ollama (if not already on your PC)
- AI models suited to your PC

## Choosing models

The launcher **automatically recommends** models that fit your GPU. If unsure, just go with the recommendation. If you try to pick a set that is too heavy, a warning appears.

| Profile | Models | Guideline |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `low`  | gemma4:e2b, gemma4:e4b | Smaller GPUs / CPU-centric PCs |
| `mid`  | + qwen3:14b | 11 GB+ VRAM |
| `high` | + gemma4:26b | 20 GB+ VRAM |

:::caution
Models that are too heavy can take a very long time to generate or appear frozen. If unsure, start with the recommendation.
:::

## Storage locations

You can choose where data and models are stored on the first-run screen.

The defaults are fine as they are.

## Start / Stop

- **Start**: `Role Garden WebUI Launcher.exe`
- **Stop**: the stop button in the launcher
