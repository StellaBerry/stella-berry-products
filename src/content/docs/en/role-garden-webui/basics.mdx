Things go smoothly if you create these three in order.

<Steps>

1. Create a **world** (decide the place and atmosphere your character lives in)
2. Create **your character** (like an SNS account you operate)
3. Create **partner characters** (AI characters who join the timeline and DMs)

</Steps>

After that, move your story forward through the timeline and DMs. See below for details on each screen.
