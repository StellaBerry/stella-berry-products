Thank you for stopping by.
**Role Garden WebUI** is a Windows app for enjoying conversations with characters, an SNS-style timeline, and DM roleplay — all driven by a local AI that runs only on your PC.

Create your own character like an SNS account, and interact with the other characters tied to it through the timeline and DMs. Nothing you type and nothing that gets generated ever leaves your PC, so you can grow your stories with total peace of mind.
